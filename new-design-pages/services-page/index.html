Hosting, Maintenance & Support

Designpluz prides itself on the ongoing relationships we build with our clients, which is why we offer an all-inclusive hosting, maintenance, and support service for ease-of-mind and maintenance of your premium website.

We host our websites in Amazon Web Services (AWS) to ensure ultra-fast load times and a seamless user experience. Our hosting package includes backups, SSL certificates, geolocation-appropriate servers, and 99.99% uptime.

We provide ongoing maintenance to keep your website’s software updated and to monitor security, forms, integrations and more.

With our support service, you can simply email or call us with any changes you’d like, and we’ll handle them promptly. Support covers a wide range of services including content updates, form data dumps, blog or news post uploads, graphic design, page updates and more.

We provide a simple and reliable support service to help keep your website up to date with new content, images, blog or news posts and SEO ranking reports.

Our Approach

We approach website design and development from a business first perspective with prospects of providing lasting return on investment and brand recognition, as well as process simplification and automation.

We begin every project with a comprehensive evaluation phase where we dive deep into understanding your business goals, target audience, and market positioning. Through collaborative workshopping sessions, we develop detailed user personas that guide our design and development decisions.

To ensure your website attracts the right audience, we place a strong emphasis on SEO during our planning phase. We analyse your website's current ranking (or that of your competitors) and identify opportunities for improvement. Our keyword research guides your content strategy.

Our design process starts with detailed wireframes that map out the user journey and information architecture. We then create stunning UI/UX designs that not only look beautiful but also provide an intuitive and engaging user experience.
