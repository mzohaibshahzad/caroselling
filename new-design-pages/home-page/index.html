Key Considerations When Hiring a Website Design Company in Sydney

Content Management System

The first thing to look at when selecting the right agency to build your website is their knowledge and experience with Content Management Systems (CMS). At Designpluz, we have experience in every open-source CMS such as WordPress, Drupal, Wix, and so on, to achieve optimum functionality, SEO capabilities, and integration with third-party tools. We assist our clients in selecting the best CMS to sustain their long-term digital development.

Template Vs Custom Website

Templates may seem affordable, but they often limit customisation and the ability to add features you require. A custom site is built around your needs, your target audience and your brand. At Designpluz, we create custom websites and guarantee that they are 100% unique, scalable and have long-term performance.

Ability to Integrate with 3rd Party Systems

A website is more than just a look and feel; it must integrate seamlessly with all the tools your business relies on. We have expertise in integrating websites with popular third-party software applications such as Xero, Stripe, Mailchimp, MYOB, PayPal, ERP, and CRM applications. Application integration will streamline business processes, automate manual tasks and enhance the experience for both your business and your customers.

Competitor Analysis

We take a deep dive into competitive analysis before we start a new website project to see how competitors are branding their services, designing their websites and engaging with their audiences, to understand their strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities and use that information to build a website that makes you unique. We ensure that your website is not only relevant but also positioned strategically to outperform competitors by comparing it to theirs.

User Experience

A great website is defined by how users interact with it. As a reputable website design and development agency in Sydney, we always look to prioritise user experience first. Whether it is intuitive navigation, fast loading pages, responsive designs or clear calls to action, our websites let visitors use easily and almost always lean towards conversion. Using what we learn about your audience's behaviour and expectations, we build a digital experience that creates trust, enhances satisfaction and grows your business.

Our Web Design & Development Process

While our process is simple and straightforward, it provides the backbone to quality website development and achieving digital objectives. This is a proven process, it works from small websites to large websites.

Step 1
Initial Consultation

Start with a consultation led by our UI/UX expert and project managers, where we learn about your goals and share our approach. We then craft a tailored proposal designed to help your business stand out, and walk you through it in detail to ensure clarity from the beginning.

Step 2
User Interviews

Once the project begins, we run a strategy workshop to explore your user personas and plan targeted user interviews. These insights guide our design decisions and ensure the website’s structure and experience are tailored to real user needs, helping us deliver an interface that truly works.

Step 3
Client Workshopping

We conduct an in-depth workshop with key stakeholders to align on every aspect of the website. From structure and functionality to design and user experience, we cover it all. Together, we explore goals, user actions, competitor insights, brand guidelines, preferred design styles, and required integrations to ensure every detail is thoughtfully planned before development begins.

Step 4
Wireframe Prototypes

We create black and white wireframes using tools like Figma or Adobe XD to map out your website’s layout and structure. At this stage, you’ll focus on the overall feel, placement of elements, call-to-actions, headers, footers, and fonts. Your feedback is gathered during an online screen-sharing session, helping us refine and finalise the wireframes before moving into visual design. While your brand guidelines are lightly considered here, they are fully brought to life in the UI/UX stage that follows.

Step 5
UI/UX Designs

This is where your website starts to take shape. We apply your full brand guidelines, incorporating colours, imagery, and visual elements to create a high-fidelity design. While the browser-based prototype allows you to scroll and view interactions like hover states, it is not yet functional. This step gives you and your team a realistic preview of the final design, making it easy to provide feedback and ensure alignment before development begins.

Step 6
Website Development

With designs approved, we move into development on our staging server. Throughout the process, we keep you updated and share two key review points: the desktop version and then the mobile version. This is where the static designs begin to function as a real website. You’ll have the opportunity to suggest refinements like image swaps or text edits, though changes are often minimal thanks to the thorough UI/UX phase.

Step 7
Device Optimisations & Testing

After incorporating your desktop feedback, we begin optimising the website for all screen sizes and major browsers. Just before launch, you’ll have the chance to review the fully responsive site across your devices. This is the ideal time to gather any final feedback from your team before we move toward go-live.

Step 8
Web Hosting & Live

We launch your website at a quiet time, typically after business hours, to avoid disruption. Based on the website type, we may share login details or send a launch announcement. All sites are hosted on Amazon Web Services for reliable speed and security.

Step 9
Ongoing Support & Post Live Services

After launch, we continue supporting your website with tailored services that help innovative businesses grow in the digital space. Alongside initial optimisation for Google ranking, we offer ongoing SEO and Google Ads support to boost your online visibility.
